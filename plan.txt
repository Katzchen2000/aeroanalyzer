# Milestone specification

Updated on 2026-06-24.

## Current state

- Root/tip CST genome is in place and covered by tests.
- Morino panel solver is the 3D model of record.
- NeuralFoil is the runtime-default viscous backend.
- Safety gates for spar clearance, hinge moment, hardware fit, roll authority, adverse yaw, and low-confidence polars are enabled by default.

## Open milestones, in order

1. Automate AVL validation
- Compare min_drag, min_mass, and knee decks against AVL.
- Acceptance: results stay within documented tolerance bands.

2. Add dynamic stability
- Compute Dutch-roll and phugoid metrics.
- Acceptance: damping criteria can be reported or enforced.

3. Expand geometry
- Add curved planform controls and/or more than two spanwise sections.
- Acceptance: genome, seeds, lofting, and tests cover the new degrees of freedom.

4. Improve UX and analysis
- Add Pareto visualization, checkpoint/restart, sensitivity sweeps, and export helpers.
- Acceptance: runs can be resumed and inspected without manual CSV work.
